# Air-bnb-dashboard
🏠 Airbnb Listings & Reviews Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

Designed and developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Airbnb listings and customer reviews, enabling data-driven insights into pricing strategy, customer satisfaction, and market demand.

🚀 Key Highlights

Built a fully interactive dashboard analyzing listing performance, pricing trends, and review behavior

Developed DAX measures for KPIs like total listings, average price, and review scores

Implemented dynamic filtering for location, room type, and price segmentation

Transformed raw data into actionable insights using Power Query and data modeling

🛠 Tech Stack

Power BI (Dashboard Development & Visualization)

DAX (Calculated Measures & KPIs)

Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)

Data Modeling (Relationships & Optimization)

📂 Data Source

Public Airbnb dataset (Listings & Reviews)

Structured and cleaned for analytical use

💼 Business Problem

Hosts and stakeholders struggle to identify optimal pricing, understand customer sentiment, and track listing performance due to fragmented and unstructured data.

🎯 Objective

Analyze pricing distribution and demand trends

Evaluate customer satisfaction through reviews

Identify high-performing listings and locations

Support strategic decision-making using data insights

📊 Dashboard Components

KPI Cards: Total Listings, Reviews, Avg Price, Ratings

Trend Analysis: Reviews over time (Line Chart)

Comparative Analysis: Listings by location (Bar Chart)

Distribution Analysis: Room types (Donut Chart)

Geospatial Insights: Listings across regions (Map)

Detailed View: Tabular listing data

📈 Business Impact

Identified high-demand locations and pricing opportunities

Enabled hosts to optimize pricing strategies

Improved understanding of customer preferences and satisfaction trends

Supported data-backed decisions to increase bookings and revenue
